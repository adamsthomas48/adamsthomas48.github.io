Privacy Policy - P10 Racing

Effective Date: 3/21/25

1. Introduction

Welcome to P10 Racing. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you create an account:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Username

We do not collect location data, device information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation: Your name, email, and username are required to set up and manage your account.
  • Gameplay Tracking: Your username is used for league participation, leaderboards, and tracking your predictions and scores.

We do not use your data for advertising or messaging purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, and we implement Supabase RLS (Row Level Security) policies to protect user data from unauthorized access.

We retain user data until the account is deleted by the user.

5. User Rights and Control

You have full control over your data, including the right to:

  • Delete Your Account & Data: You may request to delete your account at any time, and all associated data will be removed.
  • Update or Correct Your Data: You can modify your username and email within the app.
  • Contact Us About Your Data: If you have questions or concerns about your data, you can contact us at adamthomas.atx@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties, nor do we use third-party authentication services such as Google or Apple login.

7. Compliance and Updates

We strive to follow best practices in data privacy, though we do not currently claim compliance with specific regulations such as GDPR or CCPA.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any significant changes are made, we will notify users within the app.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at adamthomas.atx@gmail.com.
