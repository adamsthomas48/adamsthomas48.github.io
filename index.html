About Me

I'm Adam Thomas, a programmer from Austin, Texas, with a passion for blending creativity and technology. My journey into web development began with photography, a hobby that taught me to appreciate composition, detail, and storytelling. That same passion for creativity naturally transitioned into designing and building websites, where I could bring ideas to life in a whole new way.

Beyond web design, I'm an avid fan of hockey and Formula 1. There's something about the precision, strategy, and excitement of these sports that resonates with me and inspires my work. Whether it's capturing the perfect layout or coding something dynamic, I strive for the same energy and attention to detail.
